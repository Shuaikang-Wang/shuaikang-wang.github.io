Shuaikang Wang

Master Student at PKU Robotics

Email: wangs@pku.edu.cn

I am a second-year master’s student at Peking University (PKU), where I am advised by Prof. Meng Guo. I received my bachelor’s degree in Robotics Engineering from the College of Engineering at Peking University in 2023. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to participate in an exchange program at the University of British Columbia (UBC). I am currently a research intern in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at National University of Singapore, working under the supervision of Prof. Xingyu Liu on robotic manipulation.

My research interests lie at the intersection of decision-making, learning, and control in robotic systems. I am particularly focused on improving the efficiency, safety, and flexibility of robots in complex tasks.
